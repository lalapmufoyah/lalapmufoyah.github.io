I'm A GIS Expert

All Things Spatial is my motto!! I have almost a decade of experience working with Geographical Information Systems. I have provided technical input to projects in both private and public sector. Fields which I've applied my knowledge and skillset include Humanitarian aid work, Epidemiology, Logistics and Transportation and W.A.S.H to mention a few. Furthermore, I have also managed to contribute my skillset in public research. Check out an example of a research paper in which I gave input.

I'm A Developer

Coding, Scripting and Programming is another side of my work routine which I enjoy periodically. I've self taught R-programming, Python, Visual Basic Scripting languages which I mainly fuse within my GIS workflow. I have an appreciable knowledge of HTML, CSS along with an appreciation of PHP programing language. My role in the humanitarian sector has allowed me to work with various Database Management Systems (DBMS) such as Postgres/PostGIS; setting up Cloud services as well as working with numerous Mobile Data Collection Kits such as Open Data Kit (ODK), KoboCollect, ArcGIS Ensuite which includes ArcGIS collector, Fieldmaps, Survey123 to mention a few. I'm A Surveyor

Surveying is my grassroot trade and it has only been over the years that I've managed to develop my other abilities and explore my potential in other disciplines within the Geo-spatial field. I am member of the Zimbabwe Institute of Geomatics as well as the Surveyors Institute of Zimbabwe which are professional associations governing the Geomatics discipline in Zimbabwe.

Engineering Surveys

I have more than 5 years experience in practising engineering surveys and providing consultancy services to atleast three firms in the Mining and Manufacturing sector. I have performed Volumetric surveys for stockpiles, Monitoring of construction works and Setting out. I have vast experience in the use of survey equipment such as Total station, Differential GPS set, Unmanned Aerial Vehicles/Drones as well as Digital levels.

Topographical Surveys

I have vast experience in carrying out topographical surveys for various accumulated over the years. Usually as a precursor to development works on a site such as construction and land development, I have been involved to map out the topography and elevation contour profiling.

Hydro-geological Surveys

I also have experience in mapping and modelling of underground hydrology profiles in with a strong link to borehole drilling. I've partaken in the mapping of lineaments, terrain modelling and give input in geo-physical surveys in an effort to establish possible borehole drill sites

I'm An Open StreetMap Volunteer

Open Data Access is a concept which fascinates me as I have experienced first-hand the banefits of access to data, moreso spatial data. This is why I took the intiative not only joining the international body of Open Street Map volunteers but also along with fellow colleagues helped set-up a local chapter of the Open Street Map community inorder to expand its reach into all persons and above all things push for the use of the mapped data in solving societal problems in the local and in the broader context.
